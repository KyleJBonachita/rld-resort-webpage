Before you pack

Good to know.

Send a quick inquiry for the latest rates, availability, policies, and inclusions.

How do I reserve a date?

Use the inquiry form or contact RLD Resort through Messenger, WhatsApp, or email. A booking is confirmed after the resort replies with the requirements.

What rooms are available?

RLD Resort offers deluxe rooms for two and larger family room arrangements with multiple beds. Availability depends on your chosen dates.

Can I book for a private event?

Yes. Include your occasion, preferred date, and estimated number of guests so the resort can discuss the best setup with you.

Are meals available?

A selection of silog meals is available. Ask the resort for the latest menu and ordering arrangements during your stay.
